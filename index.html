FAQ

Common Questions About
Mutual Fund Investing in India

What is a mutual fund and how does SIP work in India?+
A mutual fund pools money from many investors and invests in equities, debt, or hybrid instruments. A SIP lets you invest a fixed amount monthly from just Rs.500, using rupee-cost averaging to reduce timing risk. Over 10-20 years, compounding makes SIPs India's most powerful wealth-building tool.
Which is the best mutual fund to invest in India in 2025?+
There is no single best fund — it depends on your risk profile, time horizon, and goals. At Real Value, we analyze HDFC, ICICI Prudential, Nippon India, Mirae Asset, Franklin Templeton, Axis, and SBI mutual funds to build a personalized portfolio. Book a free consultation for a specific recommendation.
How much should I invest in SIP every month?+
You can start from Rs.500 per month. Ideally invest 20-30% of monthly income via SIP. A Rs.5,000 per month SIP at 12% p.a. can grow to over Rs.1 crore in 25 years through compounding. We help calculate the exact SIP amount for your goals.
What is FIRE planning and can I achieve it in India?+
FIRE means Financial Independence Retire Early — building enough corpus to live off investment returns without working. In India, this is achievable with disciplined SIPs, smart asset allocation, and long-term compounding. Real Value specializes in FIRE planning for Indian investors at all income levels.
Is Real Value Portfolio AMFI registered and safe?+
Yes. Real Value Portfolio Management is an AMFI Registered Mutual Fund Distributor ARN 24454 and official NJ India business partner. All investments are processed through SEBI-regulated fund houses. Your money is completely safe and fully regulated.
Do you serve investors outside Bharuch and Gujarat?+
Absolutely. While our office is in Bharuch, Gujarat, we serve investors pan-India including Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Surat, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, and Hyderabad. Our digital-first approach means you can get started from anywhere via WhatsApp or video call.
What is the minimum investment for mutual funds in India?+
Most mutual funds have a minimum SIP of Rs.500 per month and minimum lumpsum of Rs.1,000 to Rs.5,000. There is no upper limit. We recommend starting immediately even with a small amount — time in the market is everything when it comes to compounding wealth.
